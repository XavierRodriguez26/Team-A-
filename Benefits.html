Autonomous Vehicles offer many benefits to our saftey, emissions, and many other things!

Lower Carbon Emissions

The reduction in congestion will most likely result in a reduction of CO2 emissions as well.Since software will drive the car, the modern vehicle can now be programmed to reduce emissions to the maximum extent possible. The transition to the new-age cars is expected to contribute to a 60% fall in emissions.
